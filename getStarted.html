Birding Ethics

As a beginner birder, you may be wondering, "Are there any rules or codes of conduct?" These are the top two ethical conduct rules to keep in mind when beginning your birding journey. To find out more about birding ethics, visit Western Cape Birding.

  1. Always behave in a way that does not endager wildlife or harm the environment.
  2. Respect the rights of others in group birding situations.

Things you'll need

Getting started with birding is easy. Here are a few useful items and recources you'll need to begin your birding adventure today.

  • A love for nature
  • A good birding book like Roberts Bird Guide
  • A pair of binoculars
  • A birders society or group like SANParks
  • A camera
  • A bird-spotting checklist like the SANBI Bird Checklist
